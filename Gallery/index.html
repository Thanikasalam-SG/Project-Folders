Lion

lion, (Panthera leo), large, powerfully built cat (family Felidae) that is second in size only to the tiger; it is a famous apex predator.

Horses

The standard horse is the species Equus caballus. It was domesticated from wild horses by humans at least 5000 years ago.

Cat & Dog

The Dog is a domestic animal. It is most of the often-used animal as a pet. People prefer to have a dog as a pet rather than any other animal. The dog serves its master selflessly.

Elephant

Elephants are the biggest terrestrial animals. They are found mostly in dense forests and are always found in groups

Sheeps

The sheep is usually stockier than its relative the goat (genus Capra) and Sheep usually have short tails.

red & green Parrot

Green Turtle

The Green Turtle, also known as the Green Sea Turtle, is a species of marine turtle that lives in tropical and subtropical oceans.

Bee

Bees are flying insects of the Hymenoptera, which also includes ants, wasps and sawflies. There are about 20,000 species of bees.

Chicken

Chickens have short wings and a heavy body. This makes it hard for them to fly very far.

Rabbit

Each species is different from the next, but they all have unique traits and adaptations to help them survive

Zebras

Zebras have black fur with white stripes and predominantly white bellies. Their feet are capped with a singular, hard hoof

Panda

High in dense bamboo forests in the misty, rainy mountains of southwestern China lives one of the world's rarest mammals: the giant panda, also called the panda.

Otters

Green Parrot

Despite only having red and yellow pigments, parrots appear green due to light reflecting off their feather structures.

Gecko

Geckos are nocturnal reptiles with large eyes (the better to see you with!), and padded toes for climbing.

Cricket

Crickets are small to medium-sized insects with mostly cylindrical, somewhat vertically flattened bodies.
